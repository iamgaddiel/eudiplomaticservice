We collect information necessary for the shipment process, including but not limited to sender and recipient details, package contents, and tracking information. This information is used for providing our services and improving customer experience.

We take appropriate measures to protect the confidentiality and security of your information. This includes encryption, secure transmission methods, and restricted access to personal data within our organization.

We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, but this may affect certain functionalities.
